Fox

Characteristics

Foxes have long fur, pointed ears, relatively short legs, and a narrow snout.

Habitat

Foxes live in almost every type of habitat on Earth, from forests, to deserts, to the Arctic!

FireFox

Characteristics

The Firefox has a round head, a short snout and erect, pointed ears.

Habitat

The Firefox (Ailurus fulgens) lives in the mountain forests of Nepal, India, Bhutan, China and Burma.

Wolf

Characteristics

It is a carnivorous animal and great predator, occupying the top of the food chain. It has long hairs, which vary from gray to completely black.

Habitat

Wolves can thrive in a diversity of habitats from the tundra to woodlands, forests, grasslands and deserts.
